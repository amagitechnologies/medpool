Clinical Review Services

Precision medical document analysis executed by the exact clinical discipline your litigation requires. From foundational chronologies to highly specialized expert opinions.

Nurse Consulting & Chronologies

Executed by Veteran RNs & LNCs

The foundation of any medical case requires meticulous organization and interpretation. Our veteran Registered Nurses and Legal Nurse Consultants synthesize thousands of pages of raw medical records into compelling, fact-based timelines, identifying crucial breaches of duty and missing documentation.

  • Comprehensive Medical Chronologies & Timelines
  • Initial Merit Screenings & Case Feasibility
  • Identification of Standard of Care Deviations

Physician Expert Review

Executed by Board-Certified MDs/DOs

When cases hinge on complex causation or specialized defensibility, we deploy our exclusive network of board-certified physicians. These experts provide authoritative, deep-dive clinical analyses tailored to fortify your specific legal strategy, culminating in defensible opinions and courtroom readiness.

  • Complex Causation & Damages Analysis
  • Affidavits of Merit & Expert Reports
  • Strategic Deposition & Trial Preparation

Multi-Disciplinary Specialty Review

Executed by Specific Clinical Technologists

Not all medical evidence is captured in physician notes. For highly technical matters—such as surgical equipment failures, radiology interpretations, or specific therapy protocols—we provide reviews executed by the exact clinical discipline required, including specialized technologists, respiratory therapists, and specialized practitioners.

  • Surgical & Cath Lab Complication Analysis
  • Radiology, Imaging, & Lab Technician Reviews
  • Catastrophic Injury & Long-Term Care Audits

State & Jurisdictional Licensing

Our standard review pipeline leverages elite professionals vetted for universal clinical excellence. However, we understand that certain litigation strategies require reviewers holding active licenses in specific states (e.g., Texas, Florida, Hawaii). We source and deploy these specific jurisdictional licenses on-demand. Please note that these hyper-specific placements generally incur an administrative upcharge.
